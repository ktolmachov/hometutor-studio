### US-12.9 - Learning outcome metrics validate mastery (P1)

**As an** educational AI researcher or maintainer,
**I want** quiz, retention, transfer, SRS stability, and mastery-validation metrics,
**so that** adaptive plans and graduation claims can be audited against learning evidence.

**Acceptance:**
- Given learner sessions contain quiz, review, and mastery events,
- When I request educational or mastery-validation metrics,
- Then reports include quiz correctness, retention after 7+ days, transfer outcomes, SRS stability, and mastery correlation signals.
- And false-positive graduation cases can be identified for review.
- And persistence reads go through `app/user_state.py` boundaries, not direct SQLite connections in services.

**Related package:** `epoch-qbi-learning-metrics-validation`.
