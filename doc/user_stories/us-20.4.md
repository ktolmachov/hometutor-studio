### US-20.4 - Weak-concept recovery route

**As a** Learner who failed or struggled on a quiz,  
**I want** the next action to guide me through the weak concept,  
**so that** failure becomes a recovery step instead of a dead end.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given a quiz result marks one or more weak concepts,
- When the Smart Study Router chooses the next action,
- Then `quiz_failed` recommends "Разобрать слабое место" or equivalent recovery copy.
- And the reason references the local quiz result or weak concept signal.
- And retry, tutor explanation, flashcard creation, and dashboard remain reachable as safe alternatives.
