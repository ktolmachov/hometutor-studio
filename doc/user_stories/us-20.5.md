### US-20.5 - Post-answer learning runway

**As a** Learner who just received a sourced answer,  
**I want** the next action to turn that answer into a short learning loop,  
**so that** an answer becomes tutor, quiz, cards, or plan instead of a one-off chat.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given a recent answer has enough source and trust signal,
- When the answer surface renders the Smart Study Router card,
- Then `answer_ready` can recommend "Учить тему" or equivalent learning copy.
- And the reason explains that the answer is ready but understanding has not yet been checked.
- And source inspection, simpler explanation, tutor, quiz, flashcards, and dashboard remain reachable.
