### US-20.7 - Contrastive Router Explanation

**As a** learner,  
**I want** the router to explain why the suggested action is better than the other visible modes right now,  
**so that** I trust the recommendation without losing access to tutor, quiz, flashcards, or dashboard.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given the router recommends a primary action, the card shows a compact "why this, not X" explanation for at least one visible alternative.
- Given tutor, quiz, flashcards, and dashboard are available, the explanation does not remove or visually bury those entry points.
- Given local state is insufficient to compare alternatives, the card says that plainly and falls back to the existing reason text.
