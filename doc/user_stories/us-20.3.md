### US-20.3 - Due-review priority

**As a** Learner with due cards,  
**I want** Smart Study Router to recommend review when memory is at risk,  
**so that** spaced repetition does not disappear behind new content.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given local flashcards include due reviews,
- When the router compares due review against new tutor or quiz actions,
- Then `cards_due` can become the primary action with label "Повторить" or equivalent review copy.
- And the reason names the local due signal, such as due count or retention risk.
- And tutor, quiz, and dashboard remain available as secondary actions.
