### US-20.10 - Learner Steering Toggles

**As a** learner,  
**I want** to steer the router toward review, new learning, or gentle mode,  
**so that** the next action fits my current study capacity while still preserving pedagogical guardrails.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given the learner selects a local preference, the router stores it locally and reflects it in the next recommendation reason.
- Given the preference conflicts with a high-priority learning signal, the router explains the tradeoff rather than silently ignoring the preference.
- Given the learner clears the preference, the router returns to deterministic baseline policy.
