### US-20.6 - Accessible router and preserved entry points

**As a** keyboard or screen-reader learner,  
**I want** the recommendation, reason, and alternatives to be equally reachable,  
**so that** Smart Study Router does not become a visual-only shortcut or hide existing modes.

**Acceptance Criteria:**

- Given the Smart Study Router card renders on a learning surface,
- When the learner navigates by keyboard or screen reader,
- Then focus order reaches the primary action, reason text, and secondary actions predictably.
- And action labels are specific enough to make sense without surrounding visual context.
- And tutor, quiz, flashcards, and dashboard entry points remain available beside the recommendation.
