### US-12.10 - RAG adversarial corpus and regression gate (P1)

**As an** AI safety reviewer,
**I want** document-level adversarial RAG tests for injection, conflicting sources, and no-answer cases,
**so that** trust in answers includes evidence about what the system refuses, cites, or flags.

**Acceptance:**
- Given adversarial documents and queries are present,
- When the adversarial runner executes,
- Then it measures prompt-injection resistance, source grounding, conflicting-source behavior, and no-answer behavior.
- And failures include detailed traces for security review.
- And regression gates can fail with actionable messages when adversarial metrics degrade.
- And this remains separate from the already delivered autonomous control-plane adversarial harness.

**Related packages:** `epoch-qbi-adversarial-corpus-runner`, `epoch-qbi-adversarial-regression-gate`.
