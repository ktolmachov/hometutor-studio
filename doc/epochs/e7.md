# E7: Production & Ecosystem

Closed: **2026-04-09**

Focus: ecosystem hardening, tutor gate reliability, diagnostics, and operational
triage for regression/infra failures.

## Closure Summary

E7.0-E7.3 converted the tutor regression gate from an ad hoc check into a
production-readable CI/runbook contour:

- **E7.0:** bootstrap of the production-health direction after E6 stabilization.
- **E7.1:** tutor gate reliability hardening: deterministic `healthy` /
  `degraded` smoke payloads, `pass` / `regression_fail` / `infra_fail` status,
  and CI diagnostic summary.
- **E7.2:** eval artifact retention and triage: stable JSON report envelope,
  `--report-json`, standalone artifacts, 14-day CI retention, and runbook
  guidance for regression vs infra failures.
- **E7.3:** gate triage automation: `schema_version` 2 and `triage`
  (`next_action`, `owner_hint`, `rerun_recommended`) in reports and workflow
  summaries.

The tutor/graph/learner gate contours remain useful as CI regression guards, but
they are not active product increments by themselves.
